About Us

AskByPoll is a social polling platform which aims to provide its users a robust and dynamic medium to poll on any topic they want and get instantaneous results. AskByPoll also offers interesting reads through its blogs and trivia section. It is not only fun to vote on topics you are interested and care about but also rewarding in the sense that every activity you perform on AskByPoll helps you earn credits which you can redeem for amazing gift vouchers. Bring along your friends to earn more and Happy Polling!
