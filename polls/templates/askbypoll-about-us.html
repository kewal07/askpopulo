About

In today’s Data Driven Business Ecosystem Consumer Insights & Public Opinion form the basis for decision making and policy formulations. Theability to ‘Ask’ the people a question and measure what the majority has to say - is a tool that isfirmly embedded in the fundamentals of Decision Making.

The Power of Internet and Social Communities can empower organizations with a strong tool for Business Decisions.AskByPoll has been created with the intent to make the Opinion Gathering from Consumers Fast, Easy and Cost Effective.

Organizations can easily gather Consumer Insights using the AskByPoll Platform, at the same time use AskByPoll as a parallel engagement channel. Organizations on AskByPoll can

  • Poll & Survey on Areas of Need to gather insights from specific demographics
  • Get responses as a unique combination of Quantitative & Qualitative Intelligence
  • Distribute the Polls & Surveys on their websites, through Emails managed on the AskByPoll platform, Dedicated AskByPoll Pages and other Social Media Channels
  • Get a Real Time View of Consumer Sentimentsthrough interactive dashboards, dynamic charts & graphs

Organizations can also conduct end to end Market Research with the AskByPoll team handling all the processes from Research Design to Data Collection, Analysis and Recommendations.
