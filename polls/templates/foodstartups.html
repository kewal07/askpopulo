Analysis of Foodtech startups in India



The honeymoon period for food tech starts seems to have ended. With start-ups like Dazo and Spoonjoy shutting operations and talks about the troubles within Foodpanda and Tinyowl, both investors and consumers have started raising eyebrows.



Many players with few results

On the lines of e-commerce and hyperlocal sectors, foodtech witnessed several prominent angels and VCs funding various me-too startups that seem to have prioritised scale and growth over basic business fundamentals. We thought of looking the food tech startups from a new angle – The people’s angle.

A Primary Research on FoodTech Startups
A primary research on foodtech startups conducted on AskByPoll reveals interesting results. The research was conducted through a poll to measure which is the best foodtech startup among – Freshmenu, Swiggy, EatFresh & SpoonJoy.

Here is a snapshot of the initial result –



On drilling down further, pretty interesting insights were discovered –
• Out of the entire population, none of the females voted on SpoonJoy.
• EatFresh and SpoonJoy do not have any students in the age group of 20 – 25 voting on it.
• In the 20 – 25 agegroup, Swiggy has a 28% larger brand recall than its overall recall.
• Whereas EatFresh as a Zero Brand Recall in the 20 – 25 Age Group.
• Swiggy on the other hand takes a hit of 14% Points in the age group of 26 – 30.
