Advanced Analytics Dashboard

AskByPoll's advanced analytics dashboard gives you a macro and micro view of the activities going on your contents, reach of your content, as well as tools for detailed analysis of the polls/surveys results aggregated from multiple distribution channel like AskByPoll home page, Data widgets, Email widgets and social media sites.
